Register then login in order to start creating, stacking and learning at your own pace. StackKnot makes it easy to navigate and study multiple areas of interest.


Once you have logged in you can choose your subject and start creating sets of cards. We have Java and Javascript language options for your desired expertise.


Now your ready to stack all your knowledge and start studying. Using the whiteboard you will be provided with a text editor to type in your version of the answer then once complete it will be compared to the correct answer. You even have the ability to search other users sets of cards in both languages Java and Javascript.
